# TODO: Rewrite docs in Thomas's voice

Every doc page in the VitePress site needs to be rewritten to match the
voice and style from thomasgauvin.com blog posts.

## Voice characteristics
- Conversational, first-person ("I", "we", "you")
- "Learning by building" ethos — explain why before how
- Open with a hook that connects to the reader's experience
- Short paragraphs, direct sentences
- Natural asides with dashes — not parenthetical footnotes
- "Turns out", "pretty cool", "so, to recap" kind of phrasing
- Link generously to references
- Headers as natural thought progression, not formal outlines
- No corporate/stiff language, no "leverage", no "utilize"
